Who We Are

At M&M, we are a team of passionate professionals dedicated to bringing your brand to life through custom t-shirt printing. Specializing in B2B services

we partner with businesses across industries to create high-quality, branded apparel that stands out. Whether it's for corporate events, promotions, employee uniforms, student uniforms or retail merchandise, we provide seamless, tailor-made t-shirt printing solutions that align with your brand's identity.

Initial Consultation

Once you reach out to us, we start by understanding your specific needs. Whether it's for a promotional event, employee uniforms, student uniforms or customer giveaways, we gather key details to tailor our t-shirts to your brand. We offer various options for GSM (grams per square meter), which determines the fabric’s thickness and durability.

Customization Details

We work closely with you to choose the perfect t-shirt style for your brand—whether you prefer classic polo shirts, trendy round-neck t-shirts, or any other custom design, we ensure the fabric suits your needs. Choose from a wide range of colors to match your brand identity. We help you find the perfect shades to make your brand stand out.

Mock-ups and Approval

Once the customization is finalized, we provide you with mock-ups and samples to approve before moving forward with production. This way, you can visualize how your brand will look on the final product.

Production and Delivery

After approval, we begin the production process, ensuring top-notch quality control at every step. Once complete, your custom t-shirts are securely packaged and delivered to your doorstep.

Our Expertise

At M&M, we specialize in delivering high-quality, customized T-shirt printing services tailored for businesses, schools, and events.

Our expertise spans across every stage of the process, from fabric sourcing to on-time delivery, ensuring that your uniforms and promotional materials are crafted to perfection. Premium Fabric Selection

We source the best quality fabrics to ensure durability and comfort, offering a wide range of materials including eco-friendly options. From cotton to moisture-wicking blends, we provide the ideal fabric for employee uniforms, school attire, and promotional T-shirts.

Customization & Branding Excellence

Our team excels at bringing your brand to life through precise logo placements, ensuring each design aligns with your corporate or institutional identity. We offer a variety of printing techniques such as screen printing, embroidery, and sublimation to suit every need.

High-Capacity Production & Timely Delivery

With efficient production processes, we handle large-scale orders seamlessly. Our commitment to quality control and supply chain management ensures your products are delivered on time, every time, no matter the size of the order.

Design & Consultation Services

Whether you need a fresh design or adjustments to your existing logo, our in-house design team provides expert support. We offer mockups and prototypes to ensure you are completely satisfied before production begins.

Sustainable & Ethical Practices

We care about the environment as much as you do. That’s why we offer eco-friendly printing solutions and sustainable materials, helping your business reduce its environmental footprint while maintaining the highest quality.

Dedicated Client Support

Our B2B clients benefit from dedicated after-sales services, ensuring a smooth experience from order placement to delivery. We are here to support your needs, whether it's a one-time bulk order or an ongoing partnership.
